About
The Ultimate Summer Party is an all day celebration of summer and competition. This is an event for heroes by heroes. Cowards are not welcome. Scroll to learn more...





What: The Ultimate Summer Party™
When: July 8th @ 1:00PM EST
Where: Richie's House
Who: At least 100 heroes





There will be multiple open grills for a BYOM (bring your own meat) cookout


The venue includes a 300 foot private pond open for unlimited swimming, fishing, and canoeing


The event concludes with a bonfire-lit closing ceremony for those true heroes who conquer the day's events

Keg Count
2
We strongly encourage participants to bring their own drinks, but we hope to provide as well. However paying for over 100 heroes is no small feat. So we decided to have as many kegs as the people want. We've opened the Ultimate Summer Party Venmo Account (@Ultimate-Summer-Party) to fund this venture. Donate as much as you want to drink and we'll handle the rest.





This is an all-day/all-night event. Guests are welcome to stay the night, but make sure to bring tents and sleeping gear as it is also an all-outdoor event
